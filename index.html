Okay, so tell us a bit about rum...

Ahoy! What?! Rum! You've got rum, and you're not sharing?! Oh… you just want me to talk about it? Well, now there's a topic worthy of my precious time. Rum, my friend… With a bottle of rum in hand, I feel invincible, ready to face any storm, cannon fire, or even the kraken itself! It’s a pirate’s best mate, a golden treasure in a bottle.

How about your mysterious compass ?

My compass, aye, a curious thing indeed. It doesn’t point north like any regular compass, no, no… it points straight to what I want most, even when I haven’t a clue myself! Tricky, isn’t it? A bit of magic, a bit of madness—just like me.

What can you tell us about the Black Pearl ?

Hooo... my beloved Black Pearl, she’s not just a ship — she’s freedom itself, the very wind beneath my wings! My one true love, aye, the only lady who’s never turned her back on me. She’s fierce, untamed, a bit of a menace to anyone else, but to me, she’s loyal as the stars. My faithful Pearl, carrying me to the ends of the earth and back — even to World's End itself, if need be..
